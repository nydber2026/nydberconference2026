Registration

The Upstate NY DBER Conference brings together discipline-based education researchers from across New York State, including faculty and postdoctoral scholars from community colleges, primarily undergraduate institutions, and PhD-granting institutions. Invitations to apply have been sent to a variety of New York institutions — we encourage anyone working in DBER or STEM education research in the region to apply.

The conference provides a venue to share the range of research activities across the local NY state DBER community. These events are designed to strengthen the regional STEM education community and provide pathways toward inter-DBER and multi-institutional research.

This conference is supported by the National Science Foundation (Award #2430496). Thanks to this support, we expect to offer travel stipends to help offset participation costs (travel, hotel, and meals) for a number of attendees, as funds allow.

Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis beginning February 28, 2026. Apply here to be considered for participation and travel support.
